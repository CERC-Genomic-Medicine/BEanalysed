ROC — Quantile Sweep

In-browser port of test_ROC_perf.py. For each sheet, sweeps the negative-control quantile X, masks out rows whose LFC falls inside the [QX, Q1−X] window (unless they are positive controls), and computes AUC on the neg|rank score. The best X (max AUC) is marked.
